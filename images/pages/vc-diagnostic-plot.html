Variant Caller Diagnostic Plots

Download (vector graphics)

A diagnostic plot created to assess performance of variant callers of outbreak samples of the emerging fungal pathogen Candida auris. From top to bottom: putative functional effects predicted by SNPeff, minor allele frequency, read depth, and the reference sequence. Below, we have the phylogenetic tree on the left, with a heatmap showing the nucleotide called on each sample.
